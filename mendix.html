What is AuditON?

AuditON is an app designed to simplify and streamline the auditing process at Mendix, allowing for easier and faster acquisition of security certificates.

The design process

At the start of a new project, we follow a design sprint process, which we call the ‘prep sprint’. Here I lead workshops including user interviews, persona creation, wireframing, competitor research, and more. We align the business vision with the product owner and create prototypes and key performance indicators. The lines between team members are short, and I participate in technical sessions such as the creation of the domain model. This ensures a shared understanding of the users’ needs and the product vision.

Meet the Client

Screen Designs

The app was created to address the time-consuming and disruptive nature of audits and to improve communication and efficiency between different parties. The goal was creating an easy-to-use tool that could solve problems faced by evidence suppliers and security officers alike. AuditON features a centralized system for assigning tasks and a chat system for improved communication, replacing the need for multiple applications. The app also includes built-in validation and a more user-friendly dashboard for measuring evidence and efficiency.
