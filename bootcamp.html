About

Social Bootcamp is an Amsterdam based startup venture that connects the youth with working professionals in companies. It offers a social fitness program in which companies can workout as a team and give young people the opportunity to discover their talents and aspirations for the future.

Goal

Creating a brand from the ground up. The process started as a collaboration in shaping the vision, defining core values, and creating a visual identity that resonated with the audience. Finally, an identity system was created and applied to various products such as a website, flyers, social media & more.
See the website project here!

Brand Logo

Saying a lot with very little. It was important to communicate friendliness while staying premium and professional, and fitness related.
