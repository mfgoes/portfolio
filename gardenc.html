Garden Cottage

Garden Cottage is a lodge service started in 2017 near Khao Yai, Thailand. They offer practical and cozy housing in a peaceful & serene environment close to the National Park.

The cottages are located on Tachangtai road. Tachang meaning elephant, hence the logo. As they are still building their brand, I decided to create something fairly neutral with an emphasis on the nature surrounding the lodges. The terrain itself also contains a beautiful garden. In the future I plan on creating their entire brand identity and rebrand their website and internet presence.
