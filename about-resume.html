About Me


I love working in diverse, international teams and consider myself a jack of many trades. Over the last two years I’ve been taking a UX masterclass and made various UI/UX related products you can find in my portfolio. I finished my Bachelors in Communication and Multimedia design in Den Bosch, and later did an online course for the 'Responsive Web Design' Certification. I currently work at Crowd Media (Amsterdam) as a digital designer where I focus on creating animated product videos. Here I also design and develop landing pages for various in-house brands. Over the past decade my projects have been incorporated by a range of businesses in various countries around the world.

I’m currently looking for new challenges within Amsterdam in the visual and UI design field. Feel free to send me a message or give me a call!
